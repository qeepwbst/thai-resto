I've eaten here a few times now, and I've always come away very satisfied. The food is excellent, with a great variety of options - I've never been disappointed. Good value for money. Service is generally excellent, though on my most recent visit they were understaffed for the night. This caused a few delays and mix-ups. She also loves to chat with you and make sure you're having a good time. If you're in the area, definitely check out this restaurant! Vegetarian options: There are many vegetarian options on the menu, and you can request modifications to dishes. Dietary restrictions: There are gluten-free options, and you can request to have certain ingredients removed if they are an issue for you.
